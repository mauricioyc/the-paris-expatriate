Introduction Paris · 2026
For my family, who make everything possible. Obrigado.

I moved to Paris in 2021, carrying big plans and bigger dreams. Then I got caught up in work, in life, in the ordinary momentum of it all, and somewhere in the rush I stopped noticing the city around me.

In 2026 I was shaken. Once again I had to rebuild my life and my home, only this time without leaving, in the same city I thought I already knew. I picked up a camera and began to use photography as a way to rediscover Paris, and to learn something about myself.

These photographs, all made in 2026, look at life through the eyes of an expatriate. They sit with the solitude of living in another country, but they also hold the beauty this city keeps giving, in the small things, in the big things, and, slowly, in myself.
